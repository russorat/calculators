Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-05-15

Overview

Welcome to Financial Calculators. We take your privacy seriously and want to be completely transparent about how our service works. The most important thing to know is: we don't collect or store any of your data.

Data Collection

Our calculators (Mortgage Amortization and Percentage Difference) operate entirely in your web browser. This means:

  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • We don't store any of the numbers you input
  • We don't use cookies or any other tracking mechanisms
  • We don't collect any personal information
  • We don't share any data with third parties (because we don't collect any)

How Our Calculators Work

When you use our calculators:

  • The calculations happen instantly in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers for processing
  • Results are displayed immediately and are not saved anywhere
  • When you close or refresh the page, all entered data is cleared

Technical Details

For transparency, here are some technical details about our service:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure connections
  • We use standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript)
  • Any API calls between your browser and our server are stateless and process-only
  • We don't implement any analytics or tracking scripts

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
